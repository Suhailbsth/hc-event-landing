## âš ï¸ Troubleshooting

### Can't See My Event
- **Reason:** You haven't been assigned as an organizer yet
- **Solution:** Contact your event administrator

### Gate Already Occupied
- **Reason:** Another organizer is currently at that gate
- **Solution:** Choose a different gate or wait for them to end their session

### Check-in Failed
- **Possible Reasons:**
  - Invalid QR code
  - Attendee already checked in
  - Ticket not valid for this gate
  - Event hasn't started yet
- **Solution:** Check the error message and verify the ticket

### Session Expired
- **Reason:** Your login token expired (after 12 hours)
- **Solution:** Log in again

### Lost Connection
- **What Happens:** Your last heartbeat determines if session is still active
- **Solution:** Refresh the page, you should see your active session resume
